More about US

Summer Strides is a new summer running camp put on by us, Coach Isaiah Smith and Coach Samantha Louie. We're both former collegiate athletes who have fallen in love with coaching both cross country and track & field. Our combined experience includes sprints, jumps, hurdles, and distance running. Currently, we're assistant coaches at Monta Vista High School for cross country in the fall and track & field in the spring.

When you get to know us, you'll see that it is no secret that we love running and have a passion for showing kids just how fun it can be - that's what we're all about and that's what this program is all about! For now, Summer Strides will be available for any students entering 6th-9th grade and will be free to join for the first year. If you're interested, please fill out our interest form on the "Email List" page and you will be included on future communications.

Run Fun. Build Community. Find Your Stride.
